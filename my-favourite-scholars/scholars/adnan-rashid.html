About Sheik Adnan Rasid

Adnan Rashid, a distinguished historian with a specialization in the history of Islamic civilization, comparative religion, and Hadith literature, continues to captivate audiences worldwide with his deep-seated passion for history, poetry, travel, antiquities , books, and numismatics.
Having earned an honors degree in history from the University of London , Adnan Rashid is currently pursuing further studies to enhance his already impressive academic background. His commitment to scholarship is furthe r underscored by the Ijazahs in Hadith that he has received from several eminent scholars.
Adnan Rashid’s expertise extends to Islamic numismatics and ancient manuscripts , making him a notable figure in the fields of history and antiquities. He has engaged in debates with high-profile figures in politics, history, and Christian /Islamic theology, showcasing his articulate and well-informed perspectives.
His debates have taken place in prestigious institutions such as the University of Warsaw (Poland), the American University of Beirut (Lebanon), Trinity College Dublin, and various other universities across the UK and internationally
A familiar face on reputable media platforms like the BBC, Adnan Rashid has represented Islam and Muslims, contributing to a broader understanding of Islamic history and culture. His commitment to education and community service is evident as he serves as a Khateeb in several London mosques. Presently, he is conducting an extensive Tafsir course, elucidating the meaning of the Qur’an to diverse audiences based on the work of Ibn Kathir.
