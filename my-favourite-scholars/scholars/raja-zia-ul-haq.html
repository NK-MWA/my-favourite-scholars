About Raja Zia ul Haq

Raja Zia ul Haq is a Famous Pakistani Motivational speaker, trainer, who was born on Jun 05, 1985 in Karachi, Pakistan. Raja Zia ul Haq, the CEO of Youth Club has a Master’s degree in Information Technology from the University of Canberra, Australia. He holds over a decade of professional experience in the Media and Telecom industry. Over the years, he has conducted numerous lectures, workshops and podcasts including Maulvi With An Attitude.Learn More. >

Books

Raja Zia ul Haq, a well-known Islamic motivational speaker and CEO of Youth Club Pakistan, has not authored any books as of now. His work primarily focuses on lectures, workshops, and youth-oriented programs on topics such as spirituality, modern challenges, and personal development​​​​. If you're interested in his teachings, they are accessible through his talks and events rather than publications.
